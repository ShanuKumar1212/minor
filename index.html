Recommendation Steps

These steps should be followed by everyone to be protected from any unusual attacks to vulnerabilities.

1

Educate Yourself

Stay informed about the latest online threats and scams.
Be aware of common tactics used by cyberbullies and learn how to recognize and respond to them.
2

Think Before You Post

Consider the potential impact of your online posts before sharing them.
Avoid posting or sharing content that could be offensive or incite negative reactions..
3

Be Mindful of Personal Information

Avoid sharing sensitive personal information online.
Be cautious about the details you reveal on social media platforms, including your address, phone number, and school/work information.
4

Review Privacy Settings

Regularly check and update the privacy settings on your social media accounts.
Restrict access to your profile and limit who can see your posts and personal information.
About Agency

Our organization focused on combating and preventing online harassment. It employs a multi-faceted approach, utilizing advanced technology, psychological insights, and educational programs to address cyberbullying. The agency works collaboratively with online platforms, law enforcement, and communities to provide support for victims, raise awareness, and foster a culture of digital empathy and responsibility. Its mission is to create a safer online environment by actively intervening in instances of cyberbullying, implementing preventive measures, and promoting a positive and respectful online culture.
